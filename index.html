About Gundam

What is Gundam?

Gundam is one of the most legendary mecha franchises, debuting in 1979 with Mobile Suit Gundam. Created by Yoshiyuki Tomino and Sunrise Studios, the franchise introduced realistic warfare, deep storytelling, and iconic mobile suit designs.

The Gundam Universe

  • Universal Century (UC) – The original timeline with RX-78-2 Gundam.
  • After Colony (AC) – *Gundam Wing* and Wing Gundam Zero.
  • Cosmic Era (CE) – *Gundam SEED*, featuring high-speed battles.
  • Iron-Blooded Orphans – A modern take on war and survival.
